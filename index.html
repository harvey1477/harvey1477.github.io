Hongguang Li (李宏广)

Research: Agentic AI; Large Language Model (LLM), Conversational AI, NLP
Email: harvey2@mail.ustc.edu.cn

Profile

Head of QifuGPT-Chat Team and Senior Algorithm Expert at AI Lab, Qifu Technology (formerly 360 DigiTech), responsible for Qifu’s full credit-lifecycle Dialogue LLM (collections & telemarketing), and Member of the LLM Special Committee of the Chinese Information Processing Society of China (CIPS). Former Head of LLM Team at JF SmartInvest (9636.HK), leading a team building financial foundation AI agents; Former Applied Scientist of Conversational AI at Microsoft Xiaoice; previously NLP Engineer in Huawei’s Intelligent Automotive BU. Author of 10+ papers (ACL/EMNLP/COLING/NAACL) and holder of 7 granted Chinese invention patents.

Highlights

Key Project Achievements
  • Qifu Technology – QifuGPT-Chat: Delivered product-level end-to-end credit dialogue system; 10%+ performance improvements(77% ->89%) vs online baseline.
  • JF SmartInvest – FinSphere: Built LLM-based investment advisory agent FinSphere（九方灵犀） within 6 months; surpassed major players (e.g., 60% win rate vs East Money agent(东方财富-妙想); 65% win rate vs iWencai（同花顺-问财）), with highest per-user interaction turns.
  • Microsoft Xiaoice – COMEDY / XiaobingKnows: Built COMEDY from 0→1 in 2023, a memory-enhanced conversational system, co-developed with HKUST(GZ) and launched on the XEVA platform within 6 months; Also delivered XiaobingKnows in 2021, an open-domain QA system using only online search, before ChatGPT’s public release.
  • Huawei – Auto Assistant: Core member to the delivery of Huawei’s first-generation in-car assistant in 2020, deployed across X-Project (AITO/问界), C-Project (AVATR/阿维塔), and B-Project (BAIC/北汽).
