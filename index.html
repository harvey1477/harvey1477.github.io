Hongguang Li (李宏广)

Research: Agentic AI;Large Language Model (LLM), Conversational AI, NLP
Email: harvey2@mail.ustc.edu.cn

Profile

Head of QifuGPT-Chat Team and Senior Algorithm Expert at AI Lab, Qifu Technology (formerly 360 DigiTech), responsible for Qifu’s full credit-lifecycle Dialogue LLM (collections & telemarketing), and Member of the LLM Special Committee of the Chinese Information Processing Society of China (CIPS). Former Head of LLM Team at JF SmartInvest (9636.HK), leading a team of 15+ engineers building foundation models and financial AI agents; Former Applied Scientist of Conversational AI at Microsoft Xiaoice; previously NLP Engineer in Huawei’s Intelligent Automotive BU. Author of 10+ papers (ACL/EMNLP/COLING/NAACL) and holder of 7 granted Chinese invention patents.
